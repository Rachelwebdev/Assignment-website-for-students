Adult Health Nursing 2: Group Assignment 2

Introduction: Written assignment in Adult Health Nursing 2 is part of the formative assessment, and constitutes 20% of the total grade in the course. All the topics are from the course syllabus for the semester.

Below are the instructions and the due date for submitting your assignment.

Instructions/Guideline
  • Cover page/Title page
  • Table of content
  • Introduction
  • Main body with relevant subheadings
  • Summary/Conclusion
  • References
NOTE:
  1. The write-up must be well organized and clear with creativity ( Do not copy and paste)
  2. All written assignments must be typed and double-spaced, font size 12, with 1 inch margin
  3. Be sure to indent paragraphs
  4. Reference all materials using the APA style both in-text and on the reference list
  5. The number of pages: Not more than 10 pages
  6. Deadline for submission: February 27, 2023.

Please note that late submission will attract a penalty (Mark will be deducted)

Criteria for Grading
  • Title page
  • Introduction
  • Content/ main body (pathophysiology, manifestations, diagnostic studies, nursing and medical management, complications etc)
Presentation:
  • Organization and Clarity
  • Neatness and Creativity
  • Spelling
  • Conclusion
  • References: Books, journals, online courses
Total 20 marks
